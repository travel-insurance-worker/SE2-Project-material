Hello. We are Futuristic Travel.

Air travel for a variety of purposes,from leisure to business travel, has become part of the daily life of many people. There are various events in people's lives that eventually force them to change their predetermined plans. Air travel is a predetermined process and for many reasons, the passenger may want to change the time or cancel their trip. Due to the current facilities and conditions, returning or changing the ticket time is not an easy task and usually leads to financial loss for the passenger.

This website will help passengers to insure their travel for a small fee when buying a ticket.Consequently, they do not have to worry about canceling the ticket or changing the time and they can compensate for unforeseen damages during the trip with insurance.
